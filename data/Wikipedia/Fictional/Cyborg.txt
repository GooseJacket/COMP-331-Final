Cyborg (Victor "Vic" Stone) is a fictional comic book superhero. He first appeared in the DC Comics universe. He is best known for being a member of the Teen Titans.
Fictional character biography

He is an African American. He is part human and part cyborg (robot).
In other media

He is currently voiced by Khary Payton in the DC franchise and by Kenn Michael in the feature movies and direct-to-video movies. In the DC Extended Universe movies, the character is played by actor Ray Fisher.
