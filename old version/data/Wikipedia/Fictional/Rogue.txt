Rogue (real name Anna Marie LeBeau) is a fictional character and a popular superheroine in the Marvel Comics universe, appearing as one of the main characters of the X-Men comics. She was created by Chris Claremont and Michael Golden, and first appeared in Avengers Annual 10 in August of 1981. She is a prominent member of the X-Men.
Fictional character biography

Rogue ran away from home and was eventually taken in by Mystique and Destiny. Rogue's powers manifested with her first kiss, which she shared with a boy named Cody. Sadly, Cody was permanently rendered comatose, while Mystique indoctrinated the vulnerable Rogue into the Brotherhood of Evil Mutants.
Powers and abilities

She has the ability to absorb someone's powers by simply touching, but at risk of killing them in the process.
In other media

Since her debut in the comics, she has appeared in numerous media such as movies, television and video games. In the X-Men movies, she was portrayed by Anna Paquin.
