John Steel

John Steel (born 4 February 1941) is an English musician who is the drummer for The Animals. Having served as the band's drummer at its beginning in 1963, he is the only original bandmember playing in the current formation of The Animals. He was inducted with the band into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 1994.[1]
