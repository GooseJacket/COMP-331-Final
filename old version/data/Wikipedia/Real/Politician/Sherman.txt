James Schoolcraft Sherman (October 24, 1855 – October 30, 1912) was the 27th vice president of the United States from March 4, 1909 to October 30, 1912. He was a member of the Republican Party and served with U.S. President William Howard Taft.

Before he became vice president, he was a member of the U.S. House of Representatives from New York from 1887 to 1891 and from 1893 to 1909.

Sherman was the seventh and most recent vice president to have died in office.
